Voting Criteria

Know the rules before you vote

Eligibility

  • You can be a registered user.
  • Each voters can vote by using original proof.
  • Voting is permitted only for individuals who are 18 years old or above.

Voting Instructions

  1. Login using your registered credentials.
  2. Click on “View Candidates.”
  3. Click “Vote Now” under your preferred candidate.

Rules & Guidelines

Any attempt to manipulate or perform duplicate voting is strictly prohibited. Admins reserve the right to disqualify any user who violates the fair voting policy. Please ensure your vote reflects your honest opinion.
